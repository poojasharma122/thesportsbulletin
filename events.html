Events

Follow the league's biggest moments with our complete NFL events calendar. From season kickoff to the Lombardi Trophy presentation, these are the days every fan should mark on their calendar.

NFL Season Kickoff

Date: Thursday, September 4, 2025

The defending Super Bowl champions host the league’s opening game under the lights on Thursday night, with millions watching worldwide. The matchup typically features a powerhouse opponent, giving fans a marquee clash to launch the new season. Expect banner ceremonies, halftime celebrations, and a playoff-like atmosphere.

International Series Games

Date: October 5 – November 9, 2025

The NFL continues its international growth with games in London, Munich, and potentially São Paulo. These matchups often feature playoff-caliber teams and create unique early-morning viewing experiences for fans in North America. Coverage includes fan festivals, player appearances, and a global spotlight on the league’s international stars.

Thanksgiving Day Games

Date: Thursday, November 27, 2025

Thanksgiving and football go hand in hand. Detroit and Dallas each host their traditional games, followed by a primetime nightcap featuring one of the league’s biggest rivalries. This triple-header is a feast for fans, with wall-to-wall coverage and playoff implications for nearly every matchup.

Christmas Day Games

Date: Thursday, December 25, 2025

The NFL has turned Christmas Day into a holiday showcase. Two nationally televised games are scheduled, often featuring high-profile quarterbacks and playoff contenders. Fans get football, family, and festive storylines all in one.

Week 18 – Regular Season Finale

Date: January 3–4, 2026

The final weekend of the regular season is loaded with drama. The league flexes games into Sunday night to ensure maximum stakes, with win-and-in scenarios and seeding battles that often come down to the last snap. This is must-watch football for any fan.

NFL Playoffs – Wild Card Weekend

Date: January 10–12, 2026

Twelve teams begin their postseason run in a six-game weekend full of upsets and high drama. Saturday and Sunday feature tripleheaders, with the winners moving one step closer to the Super Bowl. Expect intense rematches and weather-affected games that can swing outcomes.

NFL Divisional Round

Date: January 17–18, 2026

The top seeds enter the fray. This is widely considered the best weekend of football all year — four games, all featuring elite teams fighting for a spot in Championship Sunday.

NFL Conference Championships

Date: January 25, 2026

The AFC and NFC titles are on the line. Two games, one Sunday, and a trip to the Super Bowl on the line. The winners punch their tickets to the biggest stage in sports.

Super Bowl LX

Date: February 8, 2026

Location: Levi’s Stadium – Santa Clara, California

The biggest sporting event on the planet. Two conference champions meet for the Lombardi Trophy, with global TV audiences in the hundreds of millions. Expect a halftime show featuring world-class performers, iconic commercials, and four quarters of football history.

NFL Scouting Combine

Date: Late February 2026 – Indianapolis, Indiana

Prospects from across the country showcase their athleticism in drills like the 40-yard dash, vertical jump, and bench press. Scouts, coaches, and GMs gather to interview players and begin shaping their draft boards.

NFL Draft

Date: April 23–25, 2026

Location: Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

The future of every franchise is shaped over three days. Round one delivers drama as teams make bold trades and surprising picks. Days two and three uncover hidden gems and future stars.

NFL Schedule Release

Date: May 2026 (Exact date TBD)

The entire schedule is unveiled live in primetime, with network specials analyzing the biggest games of the year. Fans start planning road trips, fantasy drafts, and key rivalry weekends as soon as the matchups are revealed.

NFL Preseason

Date: August 2026

Three weeks of tune-ups where rookies battle for roster spots and veterans get their final reps before the season begins. The Hall of Fame Game in Canton, Ohio kicks it all off with a nod to the game’s legends.
