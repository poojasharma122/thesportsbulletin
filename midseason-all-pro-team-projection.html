Midseason All-Pro Team Projection

As the season reaches its midpoint, we’ve assembled a projected All-Pro roster based on current performance and advanced metrics.

Offensive Standouts

Tyreek Hill leads all receivers in yards per route run and continues to terrorize defenses deep. Travis Kelce remains the gold standard at tight end, while Trent Williams anchors the league’s most efficient line.

Defensive Anchors

Micah Parsons leads the league in pressures, Myles Garrett is dominating against both run and pass, and Fred Warner is proving once again he’s the premier coverage linebacker.

Special Teams Impact

Justin Tucker and Tommy Townsend are setting the pace with elite accuracy and field-position flipping punts.
