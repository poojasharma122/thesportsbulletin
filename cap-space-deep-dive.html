Cap Space Deep Dive: Who Can Spend Big in 2025

Salary cap management shapes the future as much as play calling does. We project which teams will have the most financial flexibility heading into next offseason.

Teams with Major Spending Power

Expect rebuilding franchises like Arizona and Indianapolis to be aggressive players in free agency thanks to large cap reserves and minimal dead money.

Cap-Strapped Contenders
