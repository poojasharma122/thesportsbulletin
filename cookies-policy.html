What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us improve your experience by remembering your preferences, analyzing site performance, and delivering relevant content.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the Site to function properly. They enable core features like page navigation, secure login (if applicable), and access to protected areas. Disabling these cookies may affect how the Site works.

Performance & Analytics Cookies
These cookies collect anonymous data about how visitors use the Site, such as which pages are visited most often and how long users stay on each page. This helps us improve site usability and optimize our content.

Functional Cookies
Functional cookies remember your preferences — such as language settings or newsletter signup status — to provide a more personalized experience.

Advertising & Affiliate Cookies
We may use cookies to measure the performance of affiliate links or sponsored content. Clicking on these links may allow our partners to track purchases or engagement for commission and reporting purposes.

Third-Party Cookies

The Sports Bulletin may use trusted third-party services, such as Google Analytics, to collect and analyze site usage data. These services may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Options typically include:

  • Accepting or rejecting all cookies.
  • Deleting existing cookies from your device.
  • Receiving a notification before a cookie is stored.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some features of the Site.
