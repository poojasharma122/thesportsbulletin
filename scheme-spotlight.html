Scheme Spotlight: The Rise of Fangio-Style Defenses

Across the league, defensive coordinators are turning to Vic Fangio’s two-high shell system to slow down explosive passing attacks.

Core Principles

This scheme uses light boxes and split-safety looks to force offenses into shorter throws and sustained drives.

Teams Using It Best

Miami, Denver, and Philadelphia have successfully adopted variants of this defense, ranking near the top in explosive-play prevention.

Offensive Counters

Teams like Kansas City and San Francisco are adjusting by leaning into run game efficiency and play-action to exploit the light box looks.
