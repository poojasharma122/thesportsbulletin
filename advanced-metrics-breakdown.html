Advanced Metrics Breakdown: EPA and Success Rate Explained

Fans are hearing more about advanced stats like Expected Points Added (EPA) and Success Rate, but what do they really mean?

Why EPA Matters

EPA measures the value of every play relative to expected points, showing which teams truly add value on offense or prevent it on defense.

Success Rate Insights

This stat tracks how often plays keep an offense “on schedule,” offering context beyond simple yards gained.

Practical Takeaways

Understanding these numbers helps fans evaluate performance more accurately than box score stats, making them essential for bettors and fantasy managers alike.
